Are you sick of seeing your bikes and community belongings stolen? Is petty crime a problem in your otherwise well-to-do neighborhood? Well the crimewatcher is the system for you. Many petty crimes occur in areas will less foot traffic during work hours. The crimewatcher system ensures that someone is always watching. Some ideas of areas to deploy the cameras are the bike rack in the garage, the main entrance to a condo community, and in the shared parking garage. This system can help deter and eliminate crimes such as car break-ins, bike theft, and assault in public areas. Please show support for a system that will help reduce crime by integrating today's top technology.

Many of you may be thinking, "Sure you can get a camera, but who will be watching it?" The answer: All of you can be. There will be 24 hour access to the live feeds of all the cameras. If there is anything suspicious, an authorized person can send an alert that can help prevent the crime from following through. There are many options for how to respond to a crime as it is happening but we will put you in charge of the response plan as our system is highly configurable.

FAQ

What kind of alerting options are available?
Text messages and email are very popular when something suspicious is happening, but we can also have an alarm at the camera site to alert the criminal while he's in the act.
How much video will be recorded?
There is only so much space to store video, there will be a sliding window of the last x hours of monitoring content. There is also the capability to record clips of video as suspicious activity occurs.
What are our options for watching the events of a day?
The Crimewatcher uses computer vision technology to generate a feed of the highlights of the day. While most of the day is generally uneventful, certain visual cues gives us a signal of suspicious activity occurring and the user is able to jump to these scenes of the day.
